To generate a song you have to create a txt file with notes separated by a white space and in the given notation:

note 
or 
note,duration

E.G:
B6 
or
B6,0.7

Other examples can be found within the songs folder in the furElise.txt and swanLake.txt files.

More information about the note frequencies in: http://pages.mtu.edu/~suits/notefreqs.html
